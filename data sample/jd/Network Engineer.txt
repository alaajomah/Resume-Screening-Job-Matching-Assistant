Job summary



We’re looking for a motivated Network Engineer with 1–2 years of hands-on experience to join our team and support enterprise network monitoring and load-balancing environments.

 

What you’ll work on:



Implementing, configuring, and maintaining network monitoring solutions
Monitoring routers, switches, firewalls, and servers
Basic troubleshooting, alerts, and dashboards
Supporting application availability and performance
Perform basic troubleshooting of network issues and escalate complex problems when required.


What we’re looking for:



1–2 years of experience in networking or IT infrastructure
Good understanding of TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, VLANs
Basic knowledge of SNMP, ICMP, Syslog
Exposure to network monitoring tools (WUG, SolarWinds, PRTG, etc.)
Interest in load balancing and application delivery
Willingness to learn and grow
