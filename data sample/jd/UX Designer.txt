About the job
Summary:

At Jordan Ahli Bank, we are dedicated to revolutionizing the digital banking experience for our customers. We are seeking a talented UX Designer to join our team. The chosen candidate will play a pivotal role in shaping the user experience for Ahli Bank’s E-Channels. You will collaborate closely with cross-functional teams to design intuitive and engaging user interfaces that enhance usability and drive customer satisfaction.





Main Duties & Responsibilities:

User Interface Design

Develop intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces for Ahli Bank’s E-Channels, ensuring seamless navigation and user interaction
Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups to effectively communicate design ideas
User Research and Analysis

Conduct user research, including user interviews, surveys, and usability testing, to gather insights into user behaviors, needs, and motivations
Analyze user feedback and usage data to iterate and improve upon existing designs
Collaboration and Communication

Collaborate closely with product managers, developers, UX,UI Designers, and other stakeholders to translate business requirements and user needs into design solutions
Communicate design concepts and rationale effectively to cross-functional teams and stakeholders
Usability and Accessibility

Advocate for usability and accessibility best practices in design decisions
Conduct accessibility audits and ensure designs comply with accessibility standards and guidelines
Iterative Design Process

Iterate on designs based on feedback from usability testing, stakeholder input, and data-driven insights
Continuously refine and improve the user experience through iterative design processes
Design System Management

Contribute to the development and maintenance of a design system to ensure consistency across Ahli Mobile and other digital products
Document design guidelines and patterns to facilitate efficient design and development processes
Stay Updated on Industry Trends

Stay informed about industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies in UX/UI design and fintech
Apply new design tools and techniques to enhance design processes and outcomes




Skills & Qualifications:

• Bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design, Design & Visual Communications, or a related field

• Minimum 1 year of experience as a UX/UI Designer or similar role

• Proven track record of designing successful mobile applications

• Strong portfolio showcasing UX/UI design skills

• Experience in the fintech industry is a strong advantage

• Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD

• Excellent communication and collaboration skills
