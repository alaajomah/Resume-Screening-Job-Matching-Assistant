Company Description

Preiss Murphy A&A Education Group, a global leader in academic books and resources, provides comprehensive solutions for educational institutions and schools. The company offers standards-based curricula, textbooks, educational apps, and digital resources tailored to meet the needs of schools, educators, students, and parents.


About the Role

We are looking for a Digital Product Lead to join our team on a full-time, on-site basis. This role is ideal for a native English speaker who combines strong writing skills with strategic thinking and hands-on experience in developing educational digital products.


You will be responsible for transforming educational content into engaging digital activities and games, leading product development decisions, and ensuring that all digital outputs meet high educational and quality standards.


Key Responsibilities

Create, write, and edit high-quality educational digital content in native-level English
Transform content into interactive digital experiences, including activities and games
Lead the development of digital products from concept to launch
Conduct research and development to support innovative and effective product design
Define and select the most suitable interactivity models, user flows, and gameplay mechanics
Make product-level decisions to ensure optimal learning outcomes and user engagement
Ensure alignment between digital content, interactivities, and educational objectives
Collaborate closely with designers, developers, and academic teams on-site
Review, test, and refine digital products to enhance engagement and usability
Maintain consistency and quality across all digital products


Requirements

Native-level English speaker (written and spoken)
Proven experience in digital content writing and digital product development
Strong research and development skills
Strong understanding of educational goals and learner-centered design
Excellent organizational and communication skills


Preferred Qualifications

Experience in EdTech, educational publishing, or digital learning platforms
Knowledge of gamification and interactive learning methodologies
Experience working in cross-functional, on-site teams




What We Offer

Stable role with long-term potential as the company expands
Competitive salary based on experience 
Performance-based rewards and salary progression


Standard office working hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Sunday to Thursday
